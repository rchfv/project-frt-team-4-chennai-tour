Valluvar Kottam,Chennai

Valluvar Kottam is a monument in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, dedicated to the classical Tamil poet and philosopher Thiruvalluvar. The monument was built to honor Thiruvalluvar, who is best known for his work "Thirukkural," a revered Tamil literary work consisting of couplets that provide guidance on various aspects of life, ethics, and morality.
